Doing Laundry

1.Get dirty clothes
  1.1 Walk to bedroom
  1.2 Make sure all dirty clothes are in hamper
    1.2.1 Pick up dirty clothes from floor
    1.2.2 Put dirty clothes in hamper
  1.3 Grab hamper
  1.4 Carry hamper to the washer
2.Wash clothes
  2.1 Separate like color clothes
    2.1.1 Put white clothes in a pile
    2.1.2 Put light color clothes in a pile
    2.1.3 Put dark color clothes in a pile
  2.2 Add detergent to washer
    2.2.1 Grab detergent bottle
    2.2.2 Unscrew bottle cap
    2.2.3 Pour detergent into bottle cap
    2.2.4 Use bottle cap to measure detergent
    2.2.5 Pour measured amount of detergent into washer
  2.3 Start washer
    2.3.1 Choose washer setting to match clothes to be washed
    2.3.2 Press "Start" button on washer
  2.4 Add like color clothes to washer
    2.4.1 Pick up pile of clothes that match washer setting in step 2.3.1
    2.4.2 Put clothes in washer
    2.4.3 Clothes washer lid
    2.4.4 Wait until washing is complete
3.Dry clothes
  3.1 Grab clothes from washer
    3.1.1 Open washer lid
    3.1.2 Pull clothes out of washer
  3.2 Put clothes in dryer
    3.2.1 Open dryer door
    3.2.2 Put clothes in dryer
    3.2.3 Add dryer sheet to clothes in dryer
    3.2.4 Close dryer door
  3.3 Start dryer
    3.3.1 Choose setting on dryer to match clothes
    3.3.2 Choose amount of time for clothes to be dried
    3.3.3 Press "Start" button on dryer
    3.3.4 Wait until dryer is finished drying
4.Fold/Roll clothes
  4.1 Take clothes out of dryer
    4.1.1 Open dryer door
    4.1.2 Take clothes out of dryer
    4.1.3 Carry clothes to bedroom
    4.1.4 Put clothes on bed
  4.2 Fold and roll clothes
    4.2.1 Grab each piece of clothing individually
    4.2.2 Use hand to push out wrinkles
    4.2.3 Fold clothing item in half
    4.2.4 Roll clothing item into a tube shape
    4.2.5 Set clothing item aside in a pile
5.Put clothes away
  5.1 Separate clothes by type
    5.1.1 Put all t-shirts in a pile
    5.1.2 Put all jeans in a pile
    5.1.3 Put all socks and underwear in a pile
  5.2 Put clothes in appropriate drawer
    5.2.1 Open drawers on dresser
    5.2.2 Put each clothing item in corresponding drawers
    5.2.3 Close dresser drawers
